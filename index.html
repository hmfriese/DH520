Home Interactive Story Final Project

I am a Master's student in the Library and Information Studies program at the University of Alberta.

I'm interested in libraries in the age of the anthropocene and the spatial aspect of storytelling. I received my BA in English from the University of Calgary in 2015. I'm an avid runner, hiker, and skier. I cohabitate with a calico cat named Fig.

We respectfully acknowledge that we are creating this project in Amiskwaciwâskahikan, on Treaty 6 territory, a traditional gathering place for diverse Indigenous Peoples including the Cree, Blackfoot, Métis, Nakota Sioux, Iroquois, Dene, Ojibway/Saulteaux/Anishinaabe, Inuit, and many others whose histories, languages and cultures continue to influence our vibrant community.

This site and all assorted projects were created for submission to DH 520 at the University of Alberta during the Winter 2020 offering. This site and assorted projects were initially hosted at http://hucodev.srv.ualberta.ca/hmfriese/index.html and were moved to this Github repository for posterity in April 2021.
